The **CID** screen provides information about CUCEI's Centro Integral de Documentación (CID) and its language learning sub-center, the CAG (Centro de Aprendizaje Global). A separate **Registro Facial** screen covers the steps to register for facial recognition campus access, which is required to enter any CUCEI access point.

## About the CID

The Centro Integral de Documentación is CUCEI's main library and resource hub. It provides access to books, journals, and digital information to support students' academic and professional formation. Services offered include:

- External book loan (*préstamo externo*)
- Computer equipment loan
- Study cubicles
- Photocopying service
- Wireless internet

### Hours of operation

| Day | Hours |
|---|---|
| Monday – Friday | 8:00 – 19:45 |
| Saturday | 8:00 – 13:45 |

## CAG — Centro de Aprendizaje Global

The CAG is located on the **upper floor of the CID building** and is dedicated to self-directed language learning. Students can access a wide variety of multimedia materials and equipment to learn languages independently.

**Languages available:**

- French
- Italian
- Japanese
- Portuguese
- English
- German
- Spanish
- Russian (for foreign students)

Materials are available in multiple formats, with priority given to **digital formats**. The CAG is also supported by practicum students from the Departamento de Lenguas Modernas and scholarship recipients who assist students in their learning process.

**CAG facilities:**

- Two computer labs
- Reading areas
- Video and audio stations

## Facial recognition campus access

All CUCEI access points use **facial recognition** to control entry. Students and staff must register their photo in the system before they can enter campus.

### Registration steps

1. Go to [https://acceso.cucei.udg.mx](https://acceso.cucei.udg.mx).
2. In the **COMUNIDAD CUCEI** section, click **Subir Foto** and select whether you are a student or academic/administrative staff member.
3. Enter your SIIAU code and NIP.
4. Click **Subir foto**, follow the on-screen recommendations, take your photo, and press **Guardar**.
5. Optionally enter your email address.
6. Press **Guardar datos** to finish.

<Note>
  If you need to change your photo due to a problem, visit the **Revolución** entrance and ask the social service attendants — they are available Monday–Friday, **7:00 am – 5:00 pm**.
</Note>

<Warning>
  If you experience issues with the facial recognition system, go to the **Coordinación de Seguridad y Protección Universitaria**, located in the Rectoría building, during office hours: **9:00 am – 5:00 pm**.
</Warning>
