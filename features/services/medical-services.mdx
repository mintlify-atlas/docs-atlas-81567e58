The **Servicios Médicos** screen gives the CUCEI university community quick access to information about on-campus health clinics, additional medical services, and contact details. Data is sourced from `medical_services.json` and cached locally for offline access.

## How to find medical services in the app

1. Open the drawer menu and tap **Servicios Médicos**.
2. A description card is shown at the top: *"Aquí encontrarás información sobre los servicios médicos disponibles para la comunidad universitaria."*
3. Scroll through the three sections below for clinic listings, additional services, and contact information.

<Info>
  Pull down on the screen to refresh the cached data.
</Info>

## University clinics

The following university clinics are available to students and staff:

| Clinic | Type |
|---|---|
| Clínica Universitaria de Medicina Familiar | General / family medicine |
| Clínica Universitaria de Odontología | Dental care |
| Clínica Universitaria de Psicología | Psychological support |

## Additional medical services

Beyond the primary clinics, the following support services are also available:

- **Servicios de laboratorio** — clinical laboratory testing
- **Servicios de imagenología** — imaging studies
- **Servicios de fisioterapia** — physiotherapy and rehabilitation
