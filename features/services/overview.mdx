The **Student Services** section of CUCEI Ubícate brings together all administrative and support services that CUCEI students need throughout their academic life. From managing class absences to finding scholarships and accessing campus facilities, every service is one tap away.

## Navigating to student services

All student services are accessible through the **drawer menu** (hamburger icon in the top-left corner of the home screen). The services are split across two drawer sections: **Servicios Académicos** (Becas, Servicio Social, Servicios Escolares, CID) and **Servicios de Bienestar** (Servicios Médicos, Reconocimiento Facial).

<Info>
  Pull down on any service screen to refresh its content from the latest cached data.
</Info>

## Servicios Escolares quick reference

The **Servicios Escolares** screen is powered by `scholar_services.json` and covers four core administrative procedures:

| Section | What it covers |
|---|---|
| Justificación de faltas | Rules and steps for submitting a medical excuse (up to 20% of absences per semester) |
| Solicitud de documentos | Ordering academic documents via the SIATCE kiosk or online portal |
| Orden de pago | Downloading and paying semester fee orders through LEO Alumnos |
| Agenda | Pre-registration and course enrollment process on LEO Alumnos |

<Tip>
  Documents requested through SIATCE are held at the ventanilla for **one month** before being destroyed. Pick them up promptly after receiving your trámite receipt.
</Tip>
