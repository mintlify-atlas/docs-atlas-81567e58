The **Servicio Social** screen provides everything students need to understand, enroll in, and complete their mandatory social service. The content is organized into five sections drawn from `social_service.json`.

<Info>
  To contact the Social Service office directly from the app, tap the **Contactar Servicio Social** button at the bottom of the screen. It opens a pre-addressed email to `ussocial@cucei.udg.mx`.
</Info>

## General information

Social service is an opportunity for students to apply their academic knowledge and contribute to the well-being of the community. The program is designed to foster social responsibility and professional development through hands-on experience.

**Benefits of completing social service:**

- Practical learning in real environments
- Development of interpersonal and professional skills
- Networking opportunities and personal growth

### Program details

The program has a total duration of **480 hours**, distributed across supervised activities that include:

- Participation in community projects
- Attendance at workshops and seminars
- Preparation of periodic reports and evaluations

## Requirements

To participate in social service you must meet all of the following:

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| Active enrollment | Must be currently registered at the institution |
| Academic progress | Must have completed at least **60%** of program credits |
| Formal application | Must submit a formal request |
| Minimum GPA | Must hold a minimum average of **8.0** |

## Enrollment process

The enrollment process follows four steps:

1. **Review requirements and gather documentation** — confirm you meet all eligibility criteria and collect required documents.
2. **Submit application** — deliver online or in person.
3. **Evaluation and tutor assignment** — the office evaluates your file and assigns a supervisor.
4. **Program kickoff** — attend the initial orientation session to begin.

### Key dates

| Stage | Date |
|---|---|
| Registration window | September 1–15 |
| Program start | October 1 |
| Registration deadline | September 30 |

<Note>
  Meeting all deadlines is essential. Late submissions may delay your start date by a full semester.
</Note>

## Required documentation

Bring the following documents when submitting your application:

- Copy of student ID
- Updated academic transcript
- Letter of intent (*carta de motivos*)
- Signed application form
