---

The **Becas y Convocatorias** screen shows a live list of scholarship calls, mobility grants, and academic programs published by CUCEI's Coordinación de Internacionalización and partner organizations. Each entry includes the call title, eligible audience, application window, a short summary, and a direct link to the full convocatoria on the CUCEI website.

## How to browse scholarships in the app

1. Open the drawer menu and tap **Becas** (under "Servicios Académicos").
2. Scroll through the list of active and recent calls.
3. Tap any entry to open its full details page on the CUCEI website (`cucei.udg.mx`).

<Info>
  The list is sourced from `becas_convocatorias.json`. Pull down on the screen to refresh the cached data.
</Info>

## Finding official results

<Note>
  After applying to any official call from the Coordinación de Internacionalización, check results at the dedicated page listed in the app under **¿Postulaste a alguna convocatoria oficial?**
</Note>

All links in the app open the corresponding page on `https://www.cucei.udg.mx/es/servicios/becas-y-convocatorias/`.
