The **Articles** screen gives CUCEI students quick access to key excerpts from the *Reglamento General de Evaluación y Promoción de Alumnos* (RGEPA) — the university-wide academic regulation that governs grading, extraordinary exams, academic dismissal, and disciplinary conduct.

<Note>
  The article data is loaded from the bundled `articles.json` file and cached locally with `AsyncStorage`. Pull down on the list to force a fresh load from the bundle.
</Note>

## What's covered

The screen surfaces the following numbered articles from the RGEPA:

| Article | Topic |
|---------|-------|
| 25 | Grading rules for the extraordinary exam period |
| 27 | Requirements to be eligible for extraordinary evaluation |
| 33 | Automatic dismissal after failing to pass all owed subjects |
| 34 | Requesting a second chance after dismissal under Art. 33 |
| 35 | Permanent dismissal — no re-entry to the same program |
| 36 | Handling subjects removed by a curriculum change |
| 49 | Requesting a grade review from the department head |
| 90 | Causes of disciplinary responsibility and sanctions |

## Screen layout

The screen is built as a vertical `ScrollView` and is divided into three areas:

## Article structure

Each article card follows this layout from the JSON data:

```json
"33": {
  "incisos": [
    "El alumno que por cualquier circunstancia no logre una calificación aprobatoria...",
    "En caso de no presentarse al curso...",
    "SI INCURRISTE EN ARTÍCULO 33 Y QUIERES SEGUIR ESTUDIANDO:",
    "Consultar la fecha para el trámite en el cronograma de actividades..."
  ]
}
```

Each string in `incisos` is rendered as a separate bullet with a small quote icon.

## Extraordinary exam grading (Article 25)

This is one of the most-referenced articles. The key grading formula is:

- **80%** of the final grade comes from the extraordinary exam score.
- **40%** of the final grade comes from the ordinary period grade (note: this 40% weight applies *within* the extraordinary calculation, as described in the regulation).
- The final grade is the sum of both weighted values.

## Article 33 — Academic dismissal

<Tip>
  Article 33 is the most critical article for at-risk students. If you fail all owed subjects in an extraordinary period, dismissal is automatic. The app displays the full step-by-step instructions for requesting reinstatement via the SIIAU student portal (`escolar.cucei.udg.mx/cescolar`).
</Tip>

## Accessing the full regulation

Two buttons link to the complete RGEPA document:

1. **"Ver Reglamento Completo"** — in the top info banner.
2. **"Consultar Documento Oficial"** — at the bottom of the screen.

Both open the same URL using `Linking.openURL`:

```
https://secgral.udg.mx/sites/default/files/Normatividad_general/rgepa-oct-2017.pdf
```

## Pull-to-refresh

Pulling down on the scroll view re-loads `articles.json` from the app bundle and updates the `AsyncStorage` cache. A loading spinner with a purple gradient overlay is shown on first load.
